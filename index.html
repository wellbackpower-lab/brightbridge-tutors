FAQ

Common parent questions.

How quickly can I receive tutor recommendations?

Most enquiries receive a response within 24 hours, depending on subject, level, location, and tutor availability.

Are tutors checked before recommendation?

Yes. Tutor profiles are reviewed for teaching fit, subject coverage, stated experience, rates, and availability before we recommend them.

Can I request a replacement tutor?

Yes. If the first match is unsuitable, we help you look for another tutor based on your feedback.

Do you support online and in-person lessons?

Yes. Parents can request online lessons or in-person tuition across North, South, East, West, and Central Singapore.
